Overview

An online sprint to ship AI-powered solutions

Alibaba Cloud International invites the Department of Industrial Management, Faculty of Science, University of Kelaniya, students for a three-week, fully online AI Hackathon. Over 14 days, you'll learn the latest AI tooling, form a small team, and ship a working prototype that solves a real-world problem in any industry. Whether your idea is a video-generator, mobile app, enterprise database, or public portal, if AI sits at its core, it belongs here.

Format

Conducted entirely online with mentorship workshops.

100% Online

Team Size

Build alone or collaborate in groups of up to three.

1 – 3 Builders
Resources

The three developer tools you will master

Leverage official Alibaba Cloud developer suites to write code, design agent actions, and compile API endpoints.

Q

Qoder

Agentic IDE

An AI-native developer portal, CLI interface, and JetBrains utility. Write, refactor, and test applications securely using Quest Mode, Experts engine, and spec-driven structures.

Real Software Development
QW

QoderWork

Desktop AI Agent

Automate desktop and code tasks on the fly. Plan, execute, edit files, script browsers, and coordinate modular workflows in an isolated sandbox tailored for multi-step task resolution.

Provided to all Teams
M

MuleRun

AI Workflow Runtime

Instantly run, scale, and publish workflow networks. Connect complex APIs, models, databases, and structured inputs into robust, shareable environments.

Evaluation

How projects will be scored

Submissions are evaluated out of 100 total points across five fundamental benchmarks.

25%

Use of AI

▾

Depth, design, and consistency of the Artificial Intelligence deployment. We examine the logic, complexity, and how central AI automation is to the core problem resolution.

20%

Innovation & Originality

▾

Is the solution fresh and original? Does it tackle a real-world problem in an ingenious, unexpected way that sets it apart from traditional structures?

20%

Technical Execution

▾

Quality of source code, robustness, application stability, modular code patterns, and the integration layout of Qoder, QoderWork, or MuleRun.

20%

Impact & Feasibility

▾

Commercial value, target audience relevance, deployment scale, usability, and the validation of a credible path to a real product launch.

15%

Pitch & Demo Presentation

▾

Clarity, pacing, story structure, problem framing, product demo quality, and persuasive styling in the 3-minute video presentation and final slide deck.
